Lasagna Recipie

The standard total time to perfect this lasagna recipie is 2 hrs and 50 mins, to 3 hours. Prep time is approx. 1 hr 25 mins and cook time is 1 hr 15 mins.

Ingredients

  • Meat: pound of ground meat, 1/2 pound ground pork and 1/2 pound lean groun beef
  • Onion: diced and cooked with ground meat
  • Canned Tomatoes: 1 can tomato sauce and can of crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh Herbs: chop 2 table spoons of parsley and crush one clove of garlic
  • Sugar A dash of sugar
  • Spices and seasonings: dried basil, dried oregano, salt and black pepper
  • Noodles: uncooked or oven-ready to save time
  • Cheese: this layer is made up of cottage cheese, parmesan and later, shredded mozzarella
  • Eggs: will make the cheese layer extra creamy and binding agent

Steps

  1. Cook the meat: Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly.
    Add the onion and continue cooking until it´s translucent. Stir in the
    canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano,
    and sugar.
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until
    they´re al dente.
  3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the
    remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting
    with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  5. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about
    half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.

How Long to Cook Lasagna

In an oven preheated to 375 degrees F, this homemade lasagna should be perfectly baked in about 50 minutes
(30-40 minutes covered, 5-10 minutes uncovered)
